About Our Company

Fahari Industrial is a team of experienced control systems engineers dedicated to providing innovative automation solutions for various industries. Our expertise spans from Industrial Machineries to mobile machines, and we're committed to delivering top-notch services to our clients.

Our Benefits

What We Offer

At Fahari Industrial & Agrisolutions Limited, we pride ourselves on delivering a comprehensive range of Electrical and Automation services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our expertise spans various industries, and we are dedicated to enhancing your operations and significantly boosting productivity.

Highly Experienced Engineering Team
Our engineers bring years of experience and industry knowledge to every project.
Comprehensive Services

Cutting-edge Technology

Reliable Support

What You Get

Integrated Automation Solutions

Design, develop, and implement control systems for drill rigs, vehicles, and mobile equipment, ensuring seamless integration of electrical circuits and hardware.

Enhanced Operational Efficiency

Our expertise in coding delivers automated solutions that boost productivity and efficiency across your operations.

Advanced IoT Capabilities

Utilization of IoT technology for real-time data analytics and remote monitoring, enabling proactive maintenance and minimizing downtime.

Expert Diagnostic Support

Access to our specialized diagnostic assistance, leveraging industry-standard protocols like CAN and J1939, to optimize performance and reliability.
